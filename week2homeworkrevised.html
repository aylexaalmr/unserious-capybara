My experience in College as a Sophomore Student

by Aylexa Almanzar

If you ask me how has been my experience as a college student so far, I would say it has been both challenging and rewarding.
One of my biggest fears before starting college was not being able to make friends or meaningful connections. I've always been shy and it has been difficult for me to put myself out there. However, I've been fortunte to meet wonderful people that I connect with, share interests and support me.
If I have to be honest, last semester was the most challenging for me. It was the first time I took 5 classes and found myself being overwhelmed a couple of times. The most challenging class was, specifically, Communication Design 1. We had multiple assignments every class (which we had to bring printed), do lots of research and multiple exercises. However, it felt really rewarding at the end because I got to create really beautiful pieces for my portfolio and did things I had never done before and didn't see myself capable of doing (glad I proved myself that it wasn't that way!) What helped me to complete all my assignments was having the mentality that, eventually, I'll figure things out and it's just a moment.
I also realized that when working on a project, the hardest step is actually the beginning of it. Coming up with new ideas and developing a concept is something that takes time but when once I come up with a strong idea, it feels amazing!
I'm looking forward to the future semesters with a feeling of both anxiety and excitement. I know as I progress on my career, things will get more difficult but I see them as part of my process in order to learn and become a professional.
